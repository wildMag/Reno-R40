Inauguration du nouveau revetement
10-05-2006

The Musée d’Art Moderne Grand-Duc Jean, Mudam, in Luxembourg, has opened on 2nd July 2006. Directed by Marie-Claude Beaud, this new contemporary art venue will present a programme of temporary exhibitions open to all fields of present-day creation. Mudam is the first contemporary art museum in Luxembourg.
